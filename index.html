Miracles of the Bible

Witness God's Power, Love, and Mercy Through Scriptural Accounts

"Jesus did many other things as well. If every one of them were written down, I suppose that even the whole world would not have room for the books that would be written." - John 21:25

God's Power Revealed Through Miracles

The Bible is filled with accounts of miracles that demonstrate God's sovereignty, Jesus' divinity, and important spiritual truths for believers. These supernatural events reveal God's character and His relationship with humanity.

Biblical Truths Revealed Through Miracles

God's Love is Everlasting

Throughout Scripture, God's miracles demonstrate His unfailing love and compassion for His people, even when they are undeserving.

"The Lord appeared to us in the past, saying: 'I have loved you with an everlasting love; I have drawn you with unfailing kindness.'" - Jeremiah 31:3

Jesus is the Son of God

Jesus' miracles served as divine authentication of His identity as the Messiah and Son of God.

"But these are written that you may believe that Jesus is the Messiah, the Son of God, and that by believing you may have life in his name." - John 20:31

With God Everything is Possible

Biblical miracles show that nothing is too difficult for God, who created and sustains the universe.

"Jesus looked at them and said, 'With man this is impossible, but with God all things are possible.'" - Matthew 19:26

Faith is Essential

Miracles in the Bible often occurred in response to faith, demonstrating its vital role in our relationship with God.

"And without faith it is impossible to please God, because anyone who comes to him must believe that he exists and that he rewards those who earnestly seek him." - Hebrews 11:6

Focus on Jesus

When we keep our eyes fixed on Jesus, we can overcome obstacles that would otherwise be impossible.

"Let us run with perseverance the race marked out for us, fixing our eyes on Jesus, the pioneer and perfecter of faith." - Hebrews 12:1-2

God is Merciful

God's miracles frequently demonstrate His mercy and compassion toward those in need.

"The Lord is compassionate and gracious, slow to anger, abounding in love." - Psalm 103:8

Believers Can Do Great Things

Through the power of the Holy Spirit, Jesus' followers can continue His miraculous work.

"Very truly I tell you, whoever believes in me will do the works I have been doing, and they will do even greater things than these, because I am going to the Father." - John 14:12

Miracles From Scripture

John 2:1-11

Water to Wine

Jesus' First Miracle

At a wedding in Cana, Jesus turned water into wine when the host ran out, saving the family from embarrassment.

"What Jesus did here in Cana of Galilee was the first of the signs through which he revealed his glory; and his disciples believed in him." - John 2:11

Lesson: Jesus cares about our everyday needs and celebrations.

Jesus is the Son of God
Mark 4:35-41

Calming the Storm

Jesus Commands Nature

While Jesus and His disciples were crossing the sea, a furious storm arose. Jesus rebuked the wind and waves, bringing immediate calm.

"He got up, rebuked the wind and said to the waves, 'Quiet! Be still!' Then the wind died down and it was completely calm." - Mark 4:39

Lesson: Jesus has authority over all creation, and we can trust Him in life's storms.

With God Everything is Possible
John 11:1-44

Raising Lazarus

Victory Over Death

Jesus raised His friend Lazarus from the dead after he had been in the tomb for four days, demonstrating His power over death.

"Jesus said to her, 'I am the resurrection and the life. The one who believes in me will live, even though they die.'" - John 11:25

Lesson: Jesus has power over death and offers eternal life to believers.

God's Love is Everlasting
Matthew 14:22-33

Walking on Water

Peter's Faith Tested

Jesus walked on water during a storm. Peter stepped out in faith but began to sink when he took his eyes off Jesus.

"Immediately Jesus reached out his hand and caught him. 'You of little faith,' he said, 'why did you doubt?'" - Matthew 14:31

Lesson: When we focus on Jesus, we can do the impossible, but doubt causes us to sink.

Focus on Jesus
Luke 17:11-19

Healing Ten Lepers

Only One Returns

Jesus healed ten lepers, but only one returned to thank Him. Jesus noted the faith of the one who returned.

"Then he said to him, 'Rise and go; your faith has made you well.'" - Luke 17:19

Lesson: God's mercy extends to all, but gratitude and faith please Him.

Faith is Essential
Acts 3:1-10

Healing at Beautiful Gate

Peter and John's Miracle

Peter and John healed a lame man at the temple gate, demonstrating that Jesus' followers continue His work through the Holy Spirit.

"Then Peter said, 'Silver or gold I do not have, but what I do have I give you. In the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth, walk.'" - Acts 3:6

Lesson: Jesus' followers can do great things in His name through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Believers Can Do Great Things

The Miracle of Salvation

The greatest miracle is the transformation that occurs when someone places their faith in Jesus Christ. Through His death and resurrection, Jesus offers forgiveness of sins and eternal life to all who believe.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." - John 3:16
